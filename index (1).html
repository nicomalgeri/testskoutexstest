FAQ

Is this financial advice

No. This is a statistical tool to support decision making. Bet responsibly and never risk money you cannot afford to lose.

Does it guarantee profit

No model can. The aim is to balance growth with lower drawdowns using realistic inputs and robust simulations.

Can I add more leagues

Yes. The simulator is league‑agnostic and includes BeSoccer hooks for volume expansion.

Where is my data stored

The app processes inputs in real time. API keys are kept as platform secrets, not in the public code.
